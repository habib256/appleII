Binary Sun Dunes, Khepra IX
----------------------------

Two suns cast twin shadows across high dunes.
The sand sings when the wind is right.
Heat lines dance over the ridges.
A caravan rests near a shade sail.

Climb a safe dune and catch both suns in frame.
Drink water often.
