Sylvaria Prime
---------------

Welcome to Sylvaria Prime, the legendary planet of eternal forests!
Immense multicolored trees rise as far as the eye can see, their
crystalline leaves tinkling melodiously in the fragrant breeze.
Bioluminescent creatures flutter between the gigantic branches.

The perfect spot for a memorable selfie! The light filtered through
the canopy creates a magical atmosphere. Perfect for your Instagram feed.
But be careful not to get lost in this plant labyrinth...
