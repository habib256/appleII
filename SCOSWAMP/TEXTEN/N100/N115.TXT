T 115 The Patroller's Advice

The Patroller bursts out laughing and appreciates your honesty.

"You're looking for Courbensaule," he says. "Then go south,
then west to the crossroads.
From there, follow the path without ever straying from it and you
will reach the town."

You thank him and resume the journey.
