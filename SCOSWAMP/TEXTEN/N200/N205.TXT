T 205 Grognard, Beer and Sorcerers

Grognard orders another pitcher and tells you about the three sorcerers:

- **Gayolard** is according to him a good sorcerer.
- **Stratagus** has turned to Evil.
- **Pompatarte** is a mystery, powerful, but unpredictable.

All three are recruiting an adventurer to explore the Swamp.
