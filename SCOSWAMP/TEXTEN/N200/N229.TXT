T 229 The Red Handkerchief
The Giant's corpse lies; his companion, a Giantess, weeps kneeling.
She looks at you, desperate, without threat. She explains that someone
stole a red handkerchief made by his wife and he can't return
without finding it. You wish to help, but first, check your
belongings: do you have a Red Cape in your Backpack?
