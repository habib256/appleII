T 317 The Giant and Stratagus

You explain your mission to the Giant who scratches his head
thoughtfully. He's heard of these sorcerers called the
Masters, but he's never dealt with them. You then give
him fuller details about them and a memory soon comes back
to him. "I saw one day a magnificent garden in the middle of the
Swamp... It was north of here, a bit west, but there's
no path leading directly there. Perhaps that's where the
Garden Master lives?" You thank him and leave.
