T 333 Patroller's Advice

"I've studied botany," the Patroller replies, "and I've heard
of this bush, but I didn't know there were any in the
Scorpion Swamp. Going eastward, you'll find the
clearing where a good sorcerer called the Garden
Master lives. If anyone knows where this bush grows, it's surely
him." You thank him and you continue your path.
