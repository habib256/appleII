T 099 Stratagus's Purse

You hand over to Stratagus the Amulets conquered at the cost
of the perils of the Scorpion Swamp.
He seizes them with a snicker, leaves the room, then returns with
a small purse that appears heavy.

With a quick gesture, he tosses it to you. You hear the clinking
of coins inside.

"Now, be off!" he says. "Never come back, I'm done
with you."
