T 185 The Patroller's Advice
The Patroller seems convinced by your account.
"You are on the right path," he says calmly.
"Further east lives the Garden Master, a benevolent
and powerful sorcerer. Seek him, he can help you."

You thank him sincerely for this precious indication,
then resume your walk eastward.
