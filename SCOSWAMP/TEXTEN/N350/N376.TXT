T 376 Frog Master's Advice

You explain to him you're simply seeking to reach
Courbensaule and return from it. A surprised expression
then appears on his frog face. "Courbensaule lies
far to the north and west," he says. "But you're likable to me and
I'll give you advice. Don't follow the Will-o'-the-Wisp or
you'll endanger your life. And now, goodbye, I have
work waiting for me." He suddenly disappears and his
Frogs hop away with loud croaking.
You cast a glance around the clearing, but you
discover nothing interesting. The only path allowing
leaving the place is oriented north and you therefore take it,
retracing your steps toward the clearing where you
encountered the Swamp Orcs.
