T 358 Mission Accomplished

You take the gold he gave you and leave. You
wonder however... Have you acted rightly? The money
you've earned isn't very clean; alas! you can
no longer do anything about it... Next time you undertake an
adventure of this kind, you'll show yourself more circumspect in
choosing the masters you'll serve. In any case, for now,
you've successfully fulfilled your hardly shining mission...
