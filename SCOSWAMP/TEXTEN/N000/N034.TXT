T 034 Stone Choice
Which Magic Stone do you use against the POOL BEAST?
WITHERING: slows and weakens the creature, useful for control.
FIRE: inflicts immediate damage but may reveal your position.
TERROR: risks panicking the beast; variable effect depending on target.
Warning: using it consumes the chosen Stone; check your inventory.

C 237 USE WITHERING
C 291 USE FIRE
C 356 USE TERROR
C 209 Use nothing and change approach
