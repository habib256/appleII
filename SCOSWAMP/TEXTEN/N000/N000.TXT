000 : THE SCORPION SWAMP
========================

A Gamebook Adventure

You are a seasoned adventurer seeking glory!

The Scorpion Swamp is a cursed place that no one dares explore.
Monsters, traps and evil sorcerers await you there. But legend
speaks of a fabulous treasure hidden in its depths...

Can you survive the perils of the Swamp and discover its secrets?
Your choices will determine your fate!

Commands:
  [SPACE]  - Toggle between text and graphic mode
  [A-Z]    - Make a choice
  [Q]      - Quit the adventure
